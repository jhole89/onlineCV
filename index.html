About Me

I'm a London based developer, mathematician, and data guru creating awesome systems that integrate data storage, machine learning algorithms, and UX. I am driven by interesting challenges and continual learning. I am primarily a Data Scientist however I occasionally venture into the realms of Full Stack Development where I am seeking to expand my knowledge into. I have a slightly excessive love of open source and am a big believer in less is more (NoSQL > SQL, clean code > convoluted, lightweight > bloated, casual > suits). My interests include machine learning, artificial intelligence, maths, science, research, network theory, algorithmic design, NoSql, graph databases, and gaming.
