About Me

I'm a London based developer, mathematician, and data guru creating awesome systems that integrate data storage, machine learning algorithms, and UX. I am driven by interesting challenges and continual learning. I am primarily a Data Scientist however I occasionally venture into the realms of Full Stack Development where I am seeking to expand my knowledge into. I have a slightly excessive love of open source and am a big believer in less is more (NoSQL > SQL, clean code > convoluted, lightweight > bloated, casual > suits).

Education

University of Bath

Master of Science in Mathemtical Biology • 2011-2012

Mathematical biology is the application of advanced mathematical techniques to real world problems. This course taught me about designing machine learning algorithms to model and simulate problems such as the orbit of planetary bodies, performance of haemodialysis machines, fluid dynamics, and population-disease dynamics.

University of Sussex

Bachelor of Science in Mathematics • 2007-2010

Here I leaned about many different applications of mathematics and how they continue to define the world around us. I was able to gain great insight and experience in analysis, linear algebra, calculus, geometry, scientific computing with C, coding theory (cryptography), group and number theory, probability and statistics, and mathematical modelling in finance and industry.

Career

BAE Systems Applied Intelligence

Fulltime - Data Scientist (Consultant)• April 2016 - Present

As a Data Scientist at BAE Systems Applied Intelligence I work on ETL, architecture, analysis, and visualisation projects. I focus on open source and NoSQL/Graph systems using frameworks and technology such as ElasticSearch, Kibana, Apache Spark, HDFS, Python, and Javascript, along with DevOps tools such as Git and Jira.

BAE Systems Applied Intelligence

Fulltime - Data Analyst (Consultant)• May 2015 - April 2016

I spent most of my first year at BAE Systems AI in a lead developer role on a big data ETL solution for a global telco client. This taught me skills in Oracle 11g, Oracle Data Integrator, and bash shell. For my work on this role I was promoted to Data Scientist.

Ascot Underwriting Limited

Fulltime - Catastrophe Risk Analyst • April 2014 - May 2015

Ascot Underwriting is a speciality insurance syndicate within the Lloyds of London insurance market. I performed statistical data analysis which required in depth knowledge of the company's exposure database. During this time I developed technical skills in SQL and MS SQL 2008R2.
